# BAROK-IMMOBILIEN Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
Premium real estate management website with luxury aesthetic - elegant, trustworthy, and high-quality. Reference inspiration: High-end real estate platforms with sophisticated, timeless design.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: Dark anthracite (charcoal gray ~#2C2C2C)
- **Primary Light**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent Gold**: Warm gold #C9A86A (for CTAs, highlights, icons)
- **Secondary Accent**: Dark blue or emerald green (subtle use for depth)

## Typography
- **Headlines**: Playfair Display or Cormorant (serif, elegant)
- **Body Text**: Inter or Lato (sans-serif, clean)
- **Hierarchy**: Large, bold headlines with generous spacing; readable body text at 16-18px

## Layout System
- **Spacing**: Tailwind units of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 for consistent rhythm
- **Containers**: max-w-7xl for main content, max-w-6xl for text-heavy sections
- **Grid**: Use 3-column grids for service cards, 2-column for feature sections
- **Responsive**: Stack to single column on mobile

## Navigation
- **Header**: Sticky top navigation, logo left, menu items right
- **Logo**: "BAROK-IMMOBILIEN" text or logo placeholder top-left
- **Menu**: Home, Über Uns, Leistungen (dropdown), Kontakt
- **Mobile**: Hamburger menu with smooth slide-in

## Hero Section
- **Large Hero Image**: Premium real estate photo (modern house at sunset/elegant interior)
- **Headline**: "BAROK-IMMOBILIEN – Räume mit Zukunft" (large, Playfair Display)
- **Subheadline**: "Professionelle Immobilienverwaltung, Sanierung & Rundum-Service für Eigentümer, Vermieter und Investoren"
- **CTAs**: Two buttons with backdrop blur over image
  - Primary: "Jetzt Kontakt aufnehmen" (gold background)
  - Secondary: "Unsere Leistungen" (white/transparent with border)

## Key UX Principles
- **Trust signals**: Professional imagery, clear contact info, detailed service descriptions
- **Clarity**: Clean layouts, generous whitespace, easy-to-scan content
- **Sophistication**: Subtle animations (fade-ins on scroll), smooth transitions
- **German excellence**: All content in perfect, professional German
- **Mobile-first**: Fully responsive with touch-friendly interactions

## Content Tone
Professional, trustworthy, personal - emphasizing "Full-Service aus einer Hand" and "Wir kümmern uns um Ihre Immobilie – als wäre es unsere eigene."
